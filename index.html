Badak Lake

Badak lake is a gem of Ugam-Chatkal National Park. It is located at an altitude of 1,700 m. above sea level.It has an interesting feature of changing color depending on the time of day: in early morning it is blue-green, later at noon it becomes bluish, at sunset it becomes silvery.
